Visualization of the preferred alcoholic beverages in Sweden 2016
A graphical summary based on the official statistics of Systembolaget, which has monopoly on alcoholic beverages in Sweden. The raw data can be found here. The graph (log-log plot) shows how the Swedes consumed alcohol during 2016 in comparison to its price. Choose between showing the price in kr (kronor) or in APK (Alcohol Per Krona [ml/kr]).

Instructions:
Click on the colored square or the text of the category you want to show/hide
Click on "APK" or "Price" to switch between price and APK on the horisontal axis
Hover over any point to show which beverage it represents

The code is based on D3.js v4 and is available on GitHub
